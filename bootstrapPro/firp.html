Powerful features

Get a clear view of your financial life.

Firp is a unique solution for making budgets with expanded functionality, the way to change your method of finance management and bookkeeping. We provide a powerful tool allowing to create income and expenditure items with the detailed reports and statistics and to group them into budgets. The budgets can be both private, synchronized with family members, friends and colleagues and public allowing to plan the budget of another person in your calendar.

Firp is your next gen application reaching the higher level in personal finance management.

Meet our team

We are creative entrepreneurs.

We are here to be of help. Firp team is fond of creating useful web and mobile products to make our life better. We believe that the world is full of astonishing events that you would like to group into the budgets and share with friends. This is why we created an application of reusable budgets.

Next time you decide to go to Paris, New York, Sydney, Bangkok or Moscow, download Firp and try to create your first budget encompassing all places you visited, photos you made, links you followed – all the range of locations where you stayed, ate, had fun. If you share the budget with friends, they will be able to add it into their calendar and repeat your unforgettable journey.
